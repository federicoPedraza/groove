# Groove `0.1.6` — Planned Focus

This changelog entry captures the focus areas for `0.1.6`.

## What we will be working on

- **Commodities** improvements to streamline core day-to-day workflows.
- **Ease of access** updates to reduce friction for new and returning users.
- **Faster usage** optimizations to make common actions feel more responsive.
- **Key commands** refinement for clearer, more reliable command-driven flows.
- **Key shortcuts** expansion and polish to improve keyboard accessibility.

## Accessibility direction

The objective of `0.1.6` is to make Groove more accessible and efficient for everyone by improving discoverability, speed, and keyboard-first operation.
